I enjoy solving user-centric UI design challenges and problem-solving. My strengths lie in my strong communication skills, observational skills, thirst for learning, and being a team player. There are always multiple ways to solve a problem but giving the most feasible solution is most important. For my projects I used tools like Figma, Adobe Xd, Invision, Miro, and Trello.
